By Anika Reese A. Montemayor     |     March 9, 2023



Inferno Protection Services


The Inferno Protection Services fire service website was established in 2023 to provide readers and visitors with easy access to important fire safety information and emergency services resources. Since then, the website has become a vital tool for the fire department in most countries, helping the department to better communicate with the community and provide essential services to those in need.

In the early days of the website, it was primarily a source of information about fire safety and prevention. It provided tips on how to prevent fires, how to create a fire safety plan for your home or business, and how to use fire extinguishers. It also included news and updates about the local fire department, such as upcoming events, training sessions, and fire prevention initiatives.

As the website grew in popularity, the fire department expanded its capabilities to include online resources for emergency services. The website now includes a variety of features designed to help residents and visitors access emergency services quickly and easily. These features include:


  • An online reporting system for non-emergency incidents, such as reporting a fire hazard or requesting a smoke alarm installation.
  • A real-time emergency alert system that notifies subscribers of emergency situations via email, text message, or phone call.
  • A map-based system that displays the locations of fire stations, hydrants, and other important fire safety features in the community.
  • Online forms for requesting fire safety inspections, obtaining permits, and accessing other services
